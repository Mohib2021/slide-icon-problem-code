About

Blessings Hair Braiding

Offering the highest quality service, Fabiola is committed to a providing a great experience for all her clients! 

She is passionate, creative, and dedicated to delivering the best experience. She loves making people feel beautiful and confident. 

Fabiola is a full-service hair salon and specializes in: 

  • Perms
  • Relaxers
  • Men Haircuts
  • Press & Curl 
  • Braid Styles - Senegalese twists, both small and jumbo sizes, kinky twists, box braids, locs
  • Natural Hair Care 
  • Weave Styles 
  • Children's Hair Care & Braiding Styles 
  • Cranial for Locs, Twists & Braids 
  • All Types of Extensions

Come to Blessings Hair for a truly once-in-a-lifetime experience! We are the best braiding salon in Long Beach.
